Doctor's Details

Fun Activities

Does your dog have some dog friends? Invite them over to your house or to the park for some playtime! This way, you’ll get the chance to catch up with your friends too – and you’ll have one tired dog at the end of the day. Becoming a dog owner is a great way to meet new friends too.

Pet Hotel

A pet hotel, also known as a pet resort or luxury animal kennel, is an upscale facility that tends to the needs of clients' pets, while they are out of town. Most pet hotel owners personally enjoy the company of animals and understand their needs.

Grooming Services

Pet grooming is cleaning of the pets and taking hygienic care of the pets. ... Grooming services include bathing, nail clipping, and brushing of pet animals.

Veterinary 24/7

Veterinarians care for the health of animals and work to protect public health. They diagnose, treat, and research medical conditions and diseases of pets, livestock, and other animals.

Vaccines

Vaccines help prepare a dog's immune system to defend itself from any invasion of disease-causing organisms. Vaccines contain antigens, which mimic disease-causing organisms in a dog's immune system, but don't actually cause disease.

Training

Instructors are taught science-based training principles that reward appropriate dog behaviors instead of punishing negative ones. Positive Dog Training at home is a rewards-based approach to teaching pet parents how dogs think, learn, and communicate with others.
